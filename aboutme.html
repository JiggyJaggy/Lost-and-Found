About Lost and Found

Welcome to the Lost and Found platform of our school! This website was created to help students and faculty report and claim lost or found items within the campus.

Our Mission

We aim to provide an easy, fast, and reliable way for users to reconnect with their lost belongings. By digitizing the lost and found process, we reduce the chances of losing valuable items and promote a more responsible school community.

How It Works

  • Found something? Post it with a description and location.
  • Lost something? Search the listings or report it.
  • Contact the finder securely through the platform.

Who We Are

This project was developed by a group of students from the University of San Carlos as part of our final project. We hope this service helps make a positive difference!
